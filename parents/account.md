# Account & Password

This page covers everything related to your parent account — logging in, resetting your password, and what to do if you get locked out.

---

## Resetting Your Password

**Problem:** You forgot your password and can't log in.

**Solution:**

1. Go to the Questerix login page.
2. Click **Forgot password?** below the login button.
3. Enter the email address linked to your account.
4. Click **Send reset link**.
5. Check your email inbox — you'll receive an email from Questerix within a few minutes.
6. Click the link in the email. It takes you to a page where you can set a new password.
7. Enter your new password and confirm it.
8. Click **Save**. You're now logged in.

**Verification:** You're redirected to your dashboard after saving your new password.

::: warning Check your junk folder
Reset emails sometimes land in spam or junk. If you don't see it in your inbox after 5 minutes, check there first.
:::

---

## Changing Your Password

**Problem:** You are already logged in and want to change your password.

**Solution:**

1. Click your name or profile icon in the top-right corner.
2. Select **Settings** or **Account**.
3. Find the **Change Password** section.
4. Enter your current password, then your new password.
5. Click **Save changes**.

**Verification:** A confirmation message appears. Your next login will use the new password.

---

## Your Email Address

Your email address is set by your school's administrator. If you need to change it, contact your school admin — they can update it for you.

---

## Logging Out

To log out from any device:

1. Click your name or profile icon in the top-right corner.
2. Click **Log out**.

You'll be taken back to the login screen.

---

## I Never Received My Invitation Email

If you were told an account was created for you but you never got an email:

1. Check your spam/junk folder and search for "Questerix".
2. If it's not there, contact your school administrator and ask them to resend the invitation.
